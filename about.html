About the website

I created this website to share what I have learnt with others online. I looked online, and I couldn't find any well designed, easy to learn tutorial websites out there. I try to make everything on here unique and I try to include things that are helpful that you may not have ever thought of before. I always try to make sure that anything that is written as a tutorial can be understood from the beginner level so that anyone is able to understand my tutorials.
